01

Wooden Stool

A stool is one of the earliest forms of seat furniture. It bears many similarities to a chair. It consists of a single seat, for one person, without back or armrests (in early stools), on a base of a stool there are either one ,two, three or four legs. A stool is generally distinguished from chairs by their lack of arms and a back.
